Limited Access to Fresh Healthy Foods

>Many communities particularly in urban and low-income areas, suffer from limited access to fresh healthy foods. These areas, often referred to as food deserts, dominated by convinence stores and fast food outletsthat offer few nutritious options. This lack of access contributes to poor dietary habits, increased rates of diet-related diseases,and overall lower quality of life of residents.

How can these issues be addressed

  1. Encourage local food markets
  2. Urban agriculture

How does the solutions benefit?

  • Economic growth
  • Improved access to fresh Foods
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Community engagement
  • Educational opportunities
